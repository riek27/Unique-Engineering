Our Journey

Company History

From a small consultancy to a regional engineering powerhouse.

Unique Engineering & Consultancy Co. Ltd (UECC) was founded in November 2011 in Juba, South Sudan, with a vision to deliver world‑class engineering solutions in a post‑conflict environment. What started as a modest consultancy has grown into a multidisciplinary firm undertaking landmark projects across East Africa.

In 2015, we expanded operations by establishing Unique Construction General Contractor (UCGC) in Ethiopia, marking our first regional office. Since then, we have successfully completed over 150 projects for banks, government institutions, NGOs, and private developers.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation has earned us long‑term partnerships with leading organisations like Eco Bank, Equity Bank, KCB, and AECOM. Today, UECC is poised to extend its footprint to Sierra Leone, continuing to shape the built environment of the region.

Our Mission

To deliver sustainable, high‑quality engineering and construction services that exceed client expectations, create value for stakeholders, and contribute to the development of Africa’s infrastructure.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and innovative engineering firm in East and West Africa, known for integrity, technical excellence, and transformative projects that shape communities.
