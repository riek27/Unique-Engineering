About Our Company

About Unique Engineering & Consultancy

Founded in 2011, Unique Engineering & Consultancy Co. Ltd. has grown into a trusted engineering and construction company dedicated to delivering high-quality projects throughout South Sudan.

Our team consists of experienced engineers, consultants, project managers, and construction professionals committed to turning ideas into practical, sustainable, and innovative solutions.

From concept development and architectural planning to construction supervision and project execution, we provide comprehensive engineering services that meet international standards while addressing local needs.

Our mission is to contribute to the development of South Sudan through excellence in engineering, professionalism, and client satisfaction.
