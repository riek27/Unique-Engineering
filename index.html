About Our Company

Unique Engineering & Consultancy Co. Ltd (UECC)

Founded in November 2011 and registered in Juba, South Sudan, UECC is a privately owned engineering, construction, and consultancy firm with a growing regional presence across East Africa, including sister company Unique Construction General Contractor (UCGC) in Ethiopia.

We provide complete solutions in architectural design, structural & civil engineering, construction management, project supervision, supply of construction materials, and specialized bank fit‑outs for major financial institutions.

Our multidisciplinary team of engineers, architects, quantity surveyors, and project managers is committed to turning ideas into sustainable, high‑quality projects that meet international standards while addressing local needs.
